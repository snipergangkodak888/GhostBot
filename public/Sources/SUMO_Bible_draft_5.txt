          2. Security  Philosophy                                                   
                                                                                    
          Why  Intermediary Wallets Exist                                           
                                                                                    
          Intermediary wallets create a security checkpoint between treasury funds and final
          destination:                                                              
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            •  Obfuscates direct exposure of treasury wallet addresses to clients   
            •  Creates audit trail for every outbound transaction                   
            •  Allows verification step before funds leave our control              
            •  Limits blast radius if a client wallet is compromised or incorrect   
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          PRINCIPLE: Never send large amounts directly from treasury to external wallets.
          Always route through intermediary.                                        
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Why  Test Transactions Are Mandatory                                      
                                                                                    
          Test TXs catch mistakes before they become catastrophic:                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            •  Wrong wallet address = small loss, not total loss                    
            •  Network issues get detected on small amounts                         
            •  Client can confirm receipt before full transfer                      
            •  Creates clear handoff point with client acknowledgment               
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          PRINCIPLE: The 2 minutes spent on a test TX can save hours of panic and potentially
          unrecoverable funds.                                                      
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Why  Private Key Handling Is Strict                                       
                                                                                    
          PKs are the master keys to wallets. Leaked PKs = total loss:              
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            •  Group chats can be screenshotted, forwarded, or hacked               
            •  Not everyone in a group chat should have wallet access               
            •  Once a PK is exposed, there's no way to know if it's been copied     
            •  Only the decision maker should receive PKs - verify identity first   
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          PRINCIPLE: Treat PKs like nuclear launch codes. Private DM to verified decision
          maker. Never in groups.

===== PAGE 5 =====
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Why  Starting Balance Must Be Pinned                                      
                                                                                    
          The starting balance is the baseline for ALL fee calculations:            
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            •  Determines what counts as profit vs. return of principal             
            •  Client disputes often come from unclear starting points              
            •  Pinned message creates undeniable record both parties can reference  
            •  Prevents 'he said / she said' situations at cashout                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Re-Fill Clause                                                            
                                                                                    
          When additional funds are injected into MM after the initial starting balance:
            •  Document each injection with date, amount, and source in the spreadsheet
            •  Pin updated total balance in MM chat after each re-fill              
            •  Re-fills add to the principal baseline (not treated as profit)       
            •  At cashout: Total Principal = Starting Balance + All Re-Fills        
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          PRINCIPLE: If it's not communicated or pinned, it didn't happen. Pin starting balance
          immediately after launch and dashboard setup.

          3. Rules  &  Red  Lines                                                   
                                                                                    
          These are absolute rules. No exceptions. No judgment calls. Violating these puts client
          funds, team reputation, and potentially legal standing at risk.           
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Wallet & Key Security                                                     
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
              NEVER send private keys in group chats - Private DM to verified decision
           maker ONLY                                                               
              NEVER import or trade from the Deployer wallet unless doing final cashout
                                                                                    
              NEVER send large transfers without test TX first                      
              NEVER share treasury wallet addresses with clients - Use intermediary 
           wallets                                                                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Trading Operations                                                        
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
              NEVER leave newly injected treasury liquidity in a chart for over an hour if
           client is at a loss - 15-30 min max                                      
              NEVER counter-trade our own wallets - Add all tertiary wallets to peanuts
                                                                                    
           black list                                                               
              NEVER activate Peanuts profiles unless ready - They charge on activation
              NEVER let bots run unmonitored for extended periods - Check every 15 min
           minimum                                                                  
                                                                                    
              NEVER sell below bundle price when clipping supply into launch volume 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Client & Financial                                                        
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
              NEVER calculate fees without subtracting starting balance first       
              NEVER skip pinning the starting balance                               
                                                                                    
              NEVER process cashout without client confirming test TX - Wait for explicit
           confirmation                                                             
              NEVER promise specific returns or timelines, we control strategy execution,
           not the market or organic volume

          Daily Minimum Fees                                                        
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
           Service Level                         Daily Minimum                      
           Launch Day (includes MM + Dev/Launch) $5,000/Day + 1% of Token

          Launch Day Pricing Breakdown                                              
                                                                                    
          The $5K Launch Day fee is split as follows (useful for pricing discounts):
            •  60% Launch Income ($3,000) — Dev/deployment, bundle execution, wash  
               process                                                              
            •  40% MM Income ($2,000) — Dashboard setup, bot configuration, day 1 trading
          This breakdown allows for targeted discounts without reducing each team’s revenue
          disproportionately.                                                       
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Liquidation Fees                                                          
                                                                                    
           Fee Type                              Amount                             
                                                                                    
           Cashed-Out Token Liquidations         10%                                
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          The Core Fee Rule                                                         
                                                                                    
          RULE: 10% of liquidations IN EXCESS OF STARTING BALANCE (initial collateral +
          any re-fills) OR the Daily Minimum - whichever is GREATER on a given day. 
                                                                                    
          Fee Calculation Formula:                                                  
                                                                                    
          Profit = Final Balance - Starting Balance (initial + re-fills)            
                                                                                    
          Liquidation Fee = 10% of Profit (only if profit exists)                   
                                                                                    
          Final Fee = MAX(Liquidation Fee, Daily Minimum)                           
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Example 1: Liquidation Fee Applies (High Profit)                          
                                                                                    
            •  Service: 24/7 Trading Coverage($850 daily min)                       
            •  Starting Balance: 50 SOL (~$6,500 at $130/SOL)                       
            •  Final Balance at Cashout: $46,500

          5. Incident  Response    Protocol                                         
                                                                                    
          When things go wrong, follow these protocols. Don't improvise. Don't panic. Execute the
          checklist.                                                                
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Bot Failure                                                               
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          SYMPTOMS:                                                                 
          Bots stopped, no counter-trades executing, wallet errors                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:                                                        
            1. Check Peanuts dashboard - is profile still active?                   
            2. Check wallet SOL balances - are they depleted?                       
            3. Restart profile if needed                                            
            4. Notify client: 'Experiencing brief technical issue, monitoring now'  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          ESCALATE IF: Bots won't restart after 10 min, or issue is unknown         
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Wrong  Wallet Sells                                                       
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          SYMPTOMS:                                                                 
                                                                                    
          Counter-trading own wallets, unexpected sells                             
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:                                                        
            1. PAUSE all bots immediately                                           
            2. Check wallet exclusion list - are all tertiary wallets added?        
            3. Verify which wallets sold and calculate impact                       
            4. Add missing wallets to exclusion list                                
                                                                                    
            5. Document the incident and SOL impact                                 
            6. Resume bots only after verification                                  
                                                                                    
          ESCALATE IF: Significant treasury loss (>5 SOL)

===== PAGE 11 =====
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Chart Nukes (Major Dump)                                                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          SYMPTOMS:                                                                 
                                                                                    
          Price drops 50%+, large organic sells in notification channel, or panic in the chat
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:                                                        
            1. DO NOT panic buy immediately                                         
                                                                                    
            2. Assess: Is this organic selling or something else?                   
            3. Check: Are our wallets secure? Any unauthorized sells?               
            4. Communicate with client: 'Seeing heavy selling, monitoring closely'  
            5. Come up with a strategy – if we have heavy treasury, we should bid the chart up
               slowly to healthy levels. Set a budget, and do not continue to burn treasury quote
               beyond the projects means.                                           
                                                                                    
          ESCALATE IF: Signs of exploit, unauthorized wallet activity               
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Funds Misrouted                                                           
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          SYMPTOMS:                                                                 
                                                                                    
          Sent to wrong wallet, funds not arriving                                  
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:                                                        
            1. STOP all pending transactions immediately                            
            2. Verify transaction hash - where did funds actually go?               
            3. If sent to wrong OUR wallet: recoverable, document and retrieve      
            4. If sent to wrong EXTERNAL wallet: assess if recoverable              
            5. Document everything with screenshots and TX hashes                   
                                                                                    
            6. Notify client immediately with honest assessment                     
                                                                                    
          ESCALATE IMMEDIATELY: Any funds sent to unknown external wallet

          FULL STOP CONDITIONS: Any of these = pause all operations immediately     
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            •  Signs of wallet compromise or unauthorized transactions              
            •  Discovery of bug in our trading setup                                
            •  Client requests immediate halt                                       
                                                                                    
            •  Any situation where continuing could make things worse               
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Post-Incident                                                             
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            1. Document everything while fresh                                      
            2. Calculate exact financial impact                                     
            3. Communicate honestly with client                                     
            4. Determine if SUMO covers the loss (our error) or not (market conditions or their
               error)                                                               
            5. Update this manual if new failure mode discovered                    
                                                                                    
          Treasury Loss (Passive Bleed)                                             
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          SYMPTOMS:                                                                 
          Client passively losing treasury. Two balance updates in a row where SOL is down. No
                                                                                    
          obvious external dump or attack.                                          
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          DIAGNOSIS:                                                                
            6. Is volume bot running? (Volume bot costs treasury over time)         
            7. Are peanut settings misconfigured?                                   
            8. Check trading logs - are transactions failing?                       
            9. Is sell pressure from externals outpacing our collection?            
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          FIX:                                                                      
                                                                                    
            10. Reduce or pause volume bot                                          
            11. Adjust token sell/buy strategy to counter volume bot drain          
            12. Increase slippage tolerance if txns are failing                     
            13. Review peanut config and fix any misconfigurations
