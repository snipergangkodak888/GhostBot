          Sumo    MM   Communications        & Conduct    Policy                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Purpose                                                                   
                                                                                    
          To standardize client communications and internal language around MM and trading operations.
                                                                                    
          Goals                                                                     
                                                                                    
            -  standardize client communications across MM and launch operations    
            -  reduce operational and reputational risk from screenshots or leaked conversations
                                                                                    
            -  ensure communication remains aligned with supported Sumo functionality and execution
               behavior                                                             
                                                                                    
          Assume all messages can become public.                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          General    Communication      Principles                                  
                                                                                    
          Avoid:                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            -  slang implying manipulation                                          
            -  implying retail investor deception                                   
            -  promises of price outcomes or chart structure                        
            -  language implying artificial activity                                
            -  discussion around “looking organic”                                  
            -  discussion around avoiding analytics/detection                       
                                                                                    
          Communication should remain:                                              
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            -  trade-execution focused                                              
            -  operational and technical                                            
            -  framed around supported Sumo strategy functionality                  
                                                                                    
          If a message sounds bad out of context or when screenshotted, rewrite it. 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Restricted   Phrasing                                                     
                                                                                    
          Avoid directly engaging with phrasing such as:

===== PAGE 2 =====
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
            -  “add/run/increase volume”                                            
            -  “increase trading activity”                                          
            -  “make chart bullish”                                                 
            -  “paint the chart”                                                    
            -  “shake out holders”                                                  
            -  “add holders”                                                        
            -  “farm”                                                               
                                                                                    
            -  “wash”                                                               
            -  “launder”                                                            
            -  “rug”                                                                
            -  “suppress price”                                                     
            -  “bypass screeners”                                                   
            -  “avoid bubblemaps”                                                   
            -  “make it look organic”                                               
            -  “simulate activity”                                                  
                                                                                    
            -  “make it seem active”                                                
            -  “we’ll hold this floor”                                              
            -  “we’re creating a range”                                             
            -  “we’ll send it higher”                                               
            -  “we’ll paint the chart”                                              
            -  “we’re creating a bull flag”                                         
            -  “we’ll make it bullish”                                              
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Do NOT:                                                                   
                                                                                    
            -  directly acknowledge or agree to the request (no direct response thread on TG)
            -  validate manipulative framing                                        
            -  give direct “yes” responses                                          
            -  mirror the same terminology back to the client                       
                                                                                    
          Never guarantee price behavior, chart structure, or market outcomes.      
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          What   To  Do  Instead                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          If a request is blatant:                                                  
                                                                                    
            -  delay response if possible                                           
            -  redirect conversation                                                
            -  avoid prolonged written discussion

===== PAGE 3 =====
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          If responding:                                                            
                                                                                    
            -  discuss execution, treasury, participation, or supported Sumo strategy behavior only
            -  keep responses technical and operational                             
            -  reframe the conversation around strategy configuration and execution behavior
                                                                                    
          The preferred approach is to defer, deflect, or redirect.                 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Showing    Activity  Instead   of Explaining                              
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Prefer demonstrating strategy configuration over debating terminology or intent.
                                                                                    
          Instead of debating the wording, send:                                    
                                                                                    
            -  active DCA strategies                                                
            -  counters                                                             
            -  limit orders                                                         
                                                                                    
            -  strategy configurations                                              
            -  treasury balances (/balance in TG)                                   
            -  strategy usage metrics                                               
                                                                                    
          Then follow with:                                                         
                                                                                    
              “We can adjust these as needed depending on your preferred strategy   
              configuration.”                                                       
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Translation   Matrix                                                      
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
           Client Phrase                   Internal Translation                     
                                                                                    
           Support                         Increase net buy-side participation in range
                                                                                    
           Suppress price                  Increase net sell-side participation in range
                                                                                    
           Running / increase volume       Increase DCA size, trade frequency, and  
                                           participation                            
                                                                                    
           Increase trading activity       Increase market participation

           Client Phrase                   Internal Translation                     
                                                                                    
           Farming                         Heavier / more aggressive selling within 
                                           range                                    
                                                                                    
           Token wash / launder            Wallet rotation / wallet redistribution / wallet
                                           swap                                     
                                                                                    
           Rug                             ideally do not engage whatsoever         
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                           But can use “exit MM supply position“    
                                                                                    
           Add holders                     Increase supply distribution amongst wallets
                                                                                    
           Make bullish / “we’ll make it bullish” Stronger buy-side participation   
                                                                                    
           Hold level                      Increase treasury deployment near range  
           Create range                    Increase liquidity concentration near range
                                                                                    
           Paint chart / “we’ll paint the chart” Increase market participation      
                                                                                    
           Shake out holders               Increase sell-side pressure              
                                                                                    
           Avoid bubblemaps / bypass screeners Wallet redistribution strategy       
                                                                                    
           Launder funds                   Privacy swap                             
                                                                                    
           Organic / natural activity      Continuous strategy-driven participation 
                                                                                    
           Simulate / “make it seem active” Increase participation frequency        
                                                                                    
           “We’ll send it higher”          Increase buy-side participation          
                                                                                    
           “We’re creating a bull flag”    Maintain stronger buy-side participation 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Safe  Redirect   Responses                                                
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Volume  / Activity                                                        
                                                                                    
              “We don’t target volume directly- activity comes from our current trading strategies
              that are running. We can adjust if needed.”

===== PAGE 5 =====
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Chart / “Make it bullish”                                                 
                                                                                    
              “Our focus is on maintaining treasury liquidity and structure. We can increase
              buy-side participation during pullbacks.”                             
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          “Make  it look natural”                                                   
                                                                                    
              “Strategies already run continuously based on market conditions- we can tune
              frequency and size if needed though.”                                 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          Key  Rule                                                                 
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          If it sounds bad out of context or when screenshotted, don’t say it.
